◈ HOLOTABLE :: AR CONTROL GUIDE
MOTION CONTROL SETUP
DUAL-HAND TRACKING ACTIVE|REQUIRES CAMERA ACCESS
Left Hand — SELECT
The pink cursor tracks your left hand.

Pinch your left thumb + index finger together to click on whatever the blue cursor is aiming at.
PINCH = CLICK
Right Hand — AIM
Point your right index finger at the screen. The blue cursor tracks its tip.

Moving your hand rotates the 3D scene and hovers over element cards.
INDEX TIP = CURSOR
01 Click Camera OFF to enable the camera and start hand tracking.
02 Raise your right hand — move it to aim and rotate the holographic table.
03 Aim, then pinch left thumb + index to select an element card.
